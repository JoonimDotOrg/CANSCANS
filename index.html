CANSCANS Story

It is an educational mobile web game for a toddler and baby on Color, Alphabet, Number and Shape. Each of C, A, N, and S has game, flash cards and chart. It reads when blocks are tapped or clicked.

Magnetic board gives you an opportunity to create unlimited blocks in many colors, letters, shapes and languages. Video instruction is linked in the menu of the page. Hope it is useful for you.
